Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Thank you for using MobLapse ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

MobLapse is designed to function entirely locally on your device. We do not have servers that process your personal information, photos, or videos. We do not use third-party analytics services that track your behavior.

2. Device Permissions

To function correctly, MobLapse requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used solely for capturing photos to create your timelapse projects. These images are processed locally on your device.
  • Storage (Photos/Media/Files): Used to save your captured frames and exported timelapse videos to your device's internal storage.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this may limit the app's functionality.

3. Data Storage

All photos and videos created with MobLapse are stored locally on your device.

  • Timelapse Frames: Stored in the app's private internal storage to prevent accidental deletion.
  • Exported Videos: Saved to your device's public Movies directory so you can easily access and share them.

If you uninstall the app, the data stored in the app's private storage (timelapse frames) will be deleted by the Android system. We recommend exporting your projects before uninstalling.

4. Third-Party Services

MobLapse does not integrate with third-party ad networks or analytics platforms.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
